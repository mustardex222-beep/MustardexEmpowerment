About Mustardex

Bridging the Gap in Poultry Farming

We are on a mission to make poultry farming accessible, profitable, and sustainable for every community.

Rooted in Community, Driven by Innovation

Mustardex was founded with a simple but powerful vision: to empower rural and peri-urban communities through sustainable poultry farming. We design and manufacture affordable poultry incubators that deliver industry-leading hatch rates, and we produce high-nutrition chicken feed tailored to local conditions.

Our project goes beyond equipment. We provide training, ongoing support, and market linkages to ensure every farmer succeeds. By localizing production, we create jobs, reduce costs, and build resilient food systems from the ground up.
